Novæ is an organization made of young and innovative tech students, who love to develop powerful, well-designed and efficient solutions.

Our way of thinking

We believe in robust, extensible, and well-designed products. That’s why we created Novæ. Our goal, as a team, is to deliver the product that fit your needs. Don’t worry about the technical details – we will handle that for you.

Solid and Extensible Architecture

No matter the size of your project, it will grow – maybe really fast.
Solid foundations and a scalable architecture are required to keep pace while traffic increases. We care about providing technologies and building software that will go with you along the path.
